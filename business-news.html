SmartyHub Group Expands Multi-Sector Footprint Across Lagos

SmartyHub Group is a fast-growing Nigerian business ecosystem built around a simple idea: everyday life in a city like Lagos involves recurring, practical needs — a place to live, goods that need to move, and access to genuine health products. Rather than operate as a single company, SmartyHub Group has structured itself as a portfolio of independently registered businesses, each focused on solving one of those needs directly.

Operating Across Three Sectors

The group currently operates through three subsidiaries, each addressing a distinct part of urban life in Lagos:

  • MainTown Realty — real estate and flexible housing, offering verified listings and structured monthly rent models that reduce the pressure of paying a full year's rent upfront.
  • SmartyHub Logistics — fast, same-day delivery services built for the pace of a city where speed and reliability determine whether a business runs smoothly.
  • SmartyHub Organic — fresh fruits, NAFDAC-approved supplements, shawarma, and organic drinks, aimed at making healthy choices easier to access day to day.

What connects the three is a consistent approach: customer-focused solutions, a strong emphasis on urban convenience, and a digital-first, WhatsApp-enabled way of doing business that removes the friction of long queues, paperwork, and unclear pricing.

Recent Developments

As part of its ongoing growth strategy, SmartyHub Group is working to scale its real estate operations beyond Lagos, while continuing to strengthen its logistics infrastructure and expand the retail footprint of SmartyHub Organic.

Future Outlook

Looking ahead, SmartyHub Group's growth plans center on three areas: expansion into other major Nigerian cities, early-stage exploration of international markets — including Canada real estate listings through MainTown Realty — and continued investment in technology-driven systems to support the group's operations as it scales.
