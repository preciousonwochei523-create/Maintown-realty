About SmartyHub Group

Building solutions for everyday living across real estate, logistics, and healthy lifestyle services.

SmartyHub Group is a Nigerian-based multi-sector business ecosystem focused on delivering practical, everyday solutions across key industries. The company operates through a portfolio of brands designed to improve how people live, move, and access essential services in modern urban environments.
