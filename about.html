About Us

About MainTown Realty

Structured, transparent real estate — built for how Lagos actually rents.

MainTown Realty is a modern real estate platform built to simplify how people find, rent, and manage property in Nigeria—starting with Lagos.

We understand one simple truth: finding a home shouldn't feel like a gamble, and paying for it shouldn't break your financial stability.

What We Do

We connect verified landlords, trusted agents, and serious tenants through a structured and transparent system designed for flexible monthly rent payments, verified property listings, professional tenant screening, and clear accountability.

Our Difference

Unlike traditional real estate practices, we don't just list properties—we structure the entire rental experience. Our system is built on transparency, accountability, consistency, and protection for both landlords and tenants.

For Landlords

We help property owners earn steady rental income, access verified tenants, and maintain control over their properties with structured payment systems.

For Tenants

We provide access to legitimate homes, reduce fraud risks, and guide tenants through a clear and professional rental process.

Our Vision

To become one of the most trusted real estate platforms in Africa by building a system where renting is structured, payments are predictable, and trust is standard.

Our Promise

At MainTown Realty, we do not just facilitate transactions—we build reliable housing experiences backed by structure, clarity, and trust.
